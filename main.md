# Swiggy — CRM Business Review

## Company Overview

Swiggy is a hyperlocal food-delivery platform operating across major Indian cities.
The platform connects customers, restaurant partners, and delivery riders through a
mobile app and web dashboard. This document consolidates each department's business
review into a single CRM report.

## Sales

### Overview

The Sales team at Swiggy is responsible for driving the company's growth through strategic sales and marketing efforts. They focus on optimizing sales processes, performance metrics, and business strategies to enhance revenue generation. Their work includes:
Sales Strategy Development: The Sales team develops and implements sales strategies to attract and retain customers, driving revenue growth.
Sales Performance Monitoring: They monitor sales performance metrics to identify trends and areas for improvement, ensuring the company stays competitive.
Customer Engagement: The team engages with customers to understand their needs and preferences, tailoring their sales approach accordingly.
Market Expansion: They contribute to the company's market expansion by identifying new regions and customer segments to target.
Sales Operations Optimization: The Sales team optimizes sales operations to improve efficiency and effectiveness, contributing to the overall growth of the business.
By leveraging their expertise and understanding of the market, the Sales team at Swiggy plays a crucial role in the company's growth and success.
